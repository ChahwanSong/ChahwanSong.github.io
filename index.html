FCM-Sketch : lightweight data structure for network measurements

We observe that recent sketches for network measurements consume lots of hardware resources or even unavailable on programmable switches. Typically, most existing data-plane apps use Count-Min sketch by its simplicity albeit its poor accuracy as a trade-off.
We propose FCM-Sketch, a probabilistic data structure supporting accurate and generic network measurements such as flow size, cardinality, flow size distribution and entropy.
FCM-Sketch has a lightweight implementation on commodity programmable switches (Barefoot Tofino), and we expect FCM-Sketch will be used as a substitute for Count-Min in future data-plane applications.
